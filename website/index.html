Private by design

Privacy isn't a setting. It's the architecture.

Entavi can't leak what it never collects. Every part of the product is built to leave no trace.

No record of the call

No call logs, no metadata trail, no "who called whom." When a call ends, there's nothing left to hand over.

No account, ever

No phone number, email, or profile. Your identity is a code you create and discard - not a thing we store.

End-to-end encrypted

Audio and video are encrypted on your device. Keys never leave it, so no one in the middle can listen in.

Rotating codes

Burn a code after a single call or rotate it whenever you like. Old codes simply stop working.

Verify it's really them

Compare a per-call safety number to be certain you're talking to the right person - no impersonation.

Capture-aware

Optionally blur the call if a screen recording is detected, so private conversations stay private.

With Entavi
